Meet Fiber Max
Your gut health companion

Most nutrition apps count calories. Fiber Max tracks the one nutrient 95% of us are missing — dietary fiber — and helps you increase it gradually and sustainably.

Download on the App Store

Everything you need to improve your gut health

Personalised fiber plan

A gradual ramp-up algorithm built around your body, goals, and digestive health — increasing 2–3g per week so your gut adapts comfortably.

AI coach — Flora

Flora analyses your food logs and gut check-ins to generate daily tips and weekly insights tailored specifically to your patterns.

Track all 4 fiber types

Soluble, insoluble, resistant starch, and prebiotic fiber — each feeds different gut bacteria. Fiber Max shows you the full breakdown.

Weekly plant points

Based on the American Gut Project — track how many different plant foods you eat each week toward the evidence-based goal of 30+.

Evening check-ins

A quick daily gut check-in after 6pm. Flora spots patterns between what you eat and how you feel over time.

Barcode scanner

Scan any food product to instantly log its fiber content from a database of millions of foods.

Privacy Policy — Fiber Max

Effective date: March 27, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 27, 2026
Contact: neetadusanjh@gmail.com

1. Overview

Fiber Max ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dietary fiber tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored and processed, and your rights regarding your data. Fiber Max is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice.

2. Data we collect

2.1 Account information

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Authentication method (email/password, Apple Sign In, or Google Sign In)

2.2 Health and biometric data

  • Age and biological sex
  • Height and weight
  • Daily fiber intake goals and targets
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Digestive health conditions you choose to disclose (e.g. IBS, Crohn's disease, high cholesterol)
  • Activity level and stress levels
  • Food logs including meal times, food items, serving sizes, and fiber content
  • Evening symptom check-ins and weekly wellbeing check-ins

2.3 App usage and device data

  • Food search queries, saved favourites, streak and progress data
  • Notification preferences
  • Device type, OS version, push notification tokens

3. How we use your data

  • Personalise your fiber target and weekly ramp-up plan
  • Generate AI-powered daily tips, food suggestions, and weekly insights
  • Track your progress and surface trends in fiber intake and gut health
  • Send reminders if you enable notifications
  • Maintain your subscription status and process purchases

4. AI processing — OpenAI

Fiber Max uses OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini to generate personalised recommendations (the "Flora" features). The following data may be sent to OpenAI's API: your fiber goal, last 14 days of food logs, digestive conditions, dietary restrictions, symptom history, activity level, and stress level.

OpenAI does not use API data to train its models. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

5. Data storage — Supabase

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (SOC 2 Type II compliant). Data in transit is TLS encrypted; data at rest is encrypted at the storage level. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.

6. Subscriptions and payments — RevenueCat

Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

7. Food data sources

Food searches query USDA FoodData Central, Open Food Facts, and Edamam. No personal data is transmitted to these services.

8. Apple Health (optional)

If you connect Fiber Max to Apple Health, we may read or write fiber and nutrition data. This is optional and can be revoked in your device's Health app at any time.

9. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with Supabase (database), OpenAI (AI features), RevenueCat (subscriptions), and Apple (payments/Health). We may disclose data if required by law.

10. Data retention

Data typeRetention
Account and profile dataUntil account deletion
Food and symptom logsUntil account deletion or manual deletion
AI-generated tips (cached)24 hours (daily) / 7 days (weekly)

Delete all data: Settings → Account → Delete all data. Delete account: Settings → Account → Delete account. Both are permanent and irreversible.

11. Your rights

You may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data. Contact neetadusanjh@gmail.com — we respond within 30 days.

12. Children's privacy

Fiber Max is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

13. Changes to this policy

We will update the "Last updated" date when this policy changes. Significant changes will be notified within the app.
